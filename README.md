# PALLETS-DETECTION-AND-COUNTING
# BUSINESS PROBLEM :
A leading global pallet manufacturing company faces inventory challenges due to inaccurate counting of stacked pallets in warehouses. Manual and semi-automated methods are inefficient, causing stock discrepancies, delays, and higher costs. Traditional scanning fails to track stacked pallets, impacting real-time visibility, order fulfillment, and logistics. As operations grow, a precise and efficient inventory management solution is crucial.
